IPNI: Inter-Planetary Network Indexers

Scalable, high-performance content routing for IPFS

About IPNI

IPNI is a federated network of index nodes designed to supercharge content discovery on IPFS. Operated as a non-profit by dedicated individuals, IPNI offers a high-performance solution to content routing as storage densities increase. Unlike traditional DHTs, IPNI leverages complete routing tables for blazing-fast lookups.

IPNI Benefits

  • Ultra-fast Content Routing: Discover IPFS content with unparalleled speed and efficiency.
  • Scalability: IPNI scales effortlessly to accommodate the ever-growing IPFS network.
  • Data Bridging: Seamlessly connect IPFS to Filecoin and other major data providers.
